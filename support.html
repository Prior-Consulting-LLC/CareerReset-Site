Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get for free?

Week 1 (Vision) is completely free, including all three Experiences, journaling prompts, and access to the AI Career Coach. This lets you experience the full framework before deciding to continue.

What does the full program include?

The full program unlocks Weeks 2 through 6, giving you access to all 18 guided Experiences, comprehensive assessments, the Vision Board creator, weekly AI-guided reflection sessions, and the ability to export your complete journey.

It's a one-time purchase of $24.99 â€” no subscriptions or recurring charges.

How does the AI Career Coach work?

The AI Career Coach is powered by advanced AI that understands the VIEW Framework. It reads your journal entries and assessment responses (with your permission) to provide personalized insights and guidance specific to your career situation.

Think of it as a coaching conversation â€” you share your thoughts, and the coach helps you see patterns, ask better questions, and identify actionable next steps.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your journal entries, reflections, and assessment results are stored on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers. When you use the AI Coach, your conversation text is processed securely but is not stored after the session. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use this without the physical book?

Absolutely. The app is a complete, standalone program. While it's based on Lisa Prior's book Take Charge of Your VIEW, you don't need the book to benefit from the app. Everything you need is built into the 6-week experience.

How long does the program take?

The program is structured as a 6-week journey, with three Experiences per week. Most people spend 20-30 minutes per Experience. You can go at your own pace â€” faster or slower â€” but the weekly structure helps ideas develop naturally between sessions.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. Since the full program is a non-consumable in-app purchase, you can restore it on any device signed in to the same Apple ID. Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases."

Note: Journal entries and personal data are stored locally and will need iCloud sync enabled to transfer between devices.

What's the relationship between the app and the book?

The app brings Lisa Prior's Take Charge of Your VIEW (2nd Edition) to life as an interactive experience. It follows the same framework and structure, but adds interactive worksheets that save your progress, AI-powered coaching, progress tracking, and tools like text-to-speech and export that a printed book can't offer.
